How to Play

  • Keep guessing words until you're the last one standing.
  • Guess right and deal damage, guess wrong and take damage.
  • You take 1 damage every second, and heal damage when you guess right.
  • Yellow letters indicate that the letter is in the word in a different position.
  • Green letters indicate that the letter is in the correct position.
  • Blitz mode can be played with 2-5 players, Royale mode can be played with 6-99 players.
